Progress Features

More Apps

Download more apps, games, and emulators that are not found in the regular AltStore. 3rd party app support is included, starting with iNDS. More apps will be available in the future. 

Custom Apps

Install what you want, when you want, with clean listings, version control, and other benefits provided by AltStore. We will have a form to request apps to be added to our server. 

Temporary and Togglable

Burrito's AltStore is a soft modification to the original AltStore. This means, you can easily turn off Burrito's AltStore and go back to the original AltStore, or go back and be modded again.

How It Works

Simple setup for Windows that runs alongside AltServer.

1

Download and setup the proxy
The proxy will be the way we will modify AltStore to redirect to our servers AltStore channels. No data is sent to us.

2

Connect to the proxy on your iOS Device
Change your Wi-Fi settings to connect to the proxy running on your computer to connect to the BurritoServer channel you want to be on.

3

Download apps, games, and more
Open AltStore. The interface will change to notify you that you are running Burrito's AltStore.
